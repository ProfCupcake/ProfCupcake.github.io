Robotics, in various forms, has existed for quite a while. Precisely how long depends on what you classify as the "first" instance - clockwork automata are known to have existed for up to two thousand years, whereas the modern electric robot has only been around for about half a century. However, recently robotics has been pushed to ever greater heights, with the field producing many advancements that are on the cusp of causing great changes in various fields. With these great changes comes a great deal of issues to consider.

This site seeks to cover a couple of areas within robotics and discuss the various implications thereof. These are:

  • Self-Driving Cars
  • Automated Workers
